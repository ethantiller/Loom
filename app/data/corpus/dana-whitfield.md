# Dana Whitfield

Dana Whitfield is an entrepreneur and engineer who founded Vantage Analytics, a data
analytics company that was later acquired by Meridian Robotics.

Whitfield began her career as an engineer at Nimbus Systems, the cloud company
co-founded by Priya Raman and Owen Castellano. At Nimbus she worked on internal tools
for making sense of large volumes of operational data, developing skills and instincts
that she would later apply at her own company.

In 2008 Whitfield left Nimbus Systems to found Vantage Analytics. The company built
the Insight Suite, a set of dashboards and reporting tools that manufacturers used to
understand output from their factory equipment. Northwind Ventures backed Vantage
Analytics early, joining a portfolio that also included Quill and Aperture Media.

Ravi Anand worked at Vantage Analytics for nearly a decade before leaving to become
chief executive of Meridian Robotics. His long tenure at Vantage made him deeply
familiar with the Insight Suite.

In 2019 Meridian Robotics, led by Anand, acquired Vantage Analytics. Whitfield stayed
on for two years after the acquisition to lead the combined analytics group, ensuring
that the Insight Suite was successfully integrated with Meridian Robotics' Atlas Arm
product. After that transition she left to advise startups and teach at a regional
business school.

Whitfield is often cited as an example of a founder who built an institutional buyer's
confidence by delivering consistent product quality rather than chasing rapid growth.
