# Ashby Capital

Ashby Capital is a venture capital firm founded by Leon Ashby, who also founded the
chip company Beacon Semiconductor. Ashby Capital is one of the most important
investors in the Elmsworth technology cluster.

The firm's portfolio spans several of the region's best-known companies. Ashby
Capital provided early funding to Nimbus Systems, backed the AI lab Halcyon Labs
founded by Priya Raman, and invested in Cascade Payments, the fintech company
founded by Theo Vance.

Because Leon Ashby runs both a chip company and a venture firm, Ashby Capital's
investments often align with Beacon Semiconductor's business. Companies that use
the Photon chip frequently find a receptive investor in Ashby Capital.

Ashby Capital is frequently contrasted with Northwind Ventures, the other major
Elmsworth investor, which backed Quill, Vantage Analytics, and Aperture Media. The
two firms' portfolios divide much of the region's technology industry between them.
