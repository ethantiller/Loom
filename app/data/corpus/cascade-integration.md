# Cascade Payments Integration

Cascade Payments is a payments processing company founded by Theo Vance in 2011.
Vance built Cascade Payments after leaving Quill, where he had worked on the Quillpad
product team under Marcus Feld.

The company was funded by Ashby Capital, the venture firm run by Leon Ashby, which
had also backed Nimbus Systems and Halcyon Labs. Ashby Capital's interest in Cascade
Payments reflected its belief that financial infrastructure would increasingly run
on cloud and hardware platforms it already held positions in.

Cascade Payments' most significant technical relationship is its integration with
Nimbus Systems' Stratus platform. Cascade built Stratus-native billing APIs that allow
Stratus customers to process payments without leaving the platform. This gives Cascade
Payments access to Nimbus Systems' customer base, which includes manufacturers,
cloud-native software companies, and streaming services like Aperture Media.

Aperture Media uses Cascade Payments to process its subscriber billing. Meridian
Robotics uses Cascade to handle equipment purchase financing for Atlas Arm customers.
These relationships spread Cascade Payments deeply through the Elmsworth technology
cluster.

Theo Vance has said that the Stratus integration was the decision that allowed Cascade
to grow without building a traditional sales force: by embedding inside the platform
that Elmsworth companies already relied on, Cascade Payments acquired customers
through their existing cloud workflows rather than direct selling.

Cascade Payments is now one of the more profitable independent fintech companies in
the Elmsworth region and continues to add payment products designed specifically for
cloud-native and industrial customers.
