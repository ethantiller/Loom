# Vantage Analytics

Vantage Analytics is a data analytics company founded in 2008 by Dana Whitfield.
Before starting the company, Dana Whitfield had been an engineer at Nimbus
Systems, where she worked on tools for making sense of large volumes of
operational data.

Vantage Analytics built its business around the Insight Suite, a set of
dashboards and reporting tools that companies use to visualize their data. The
Insight Suite was popular with manufacturers who wanted to understand output from
their factory equipment.

Ravi Anand worked at Vantage Analytics before he became chief executive of
Meridian Robotics in 2015. His familiarity with the Insight Suite later helped
bridge the two companies.

In 2019 Vantage Analytics was acquired by Meridian Robotics, the robotics company
founded by Owen Castellano. Under Meridian Robotics, the Insight Suite was
bundled with the Atlas Arm so factory customers could analyze robot performance
directly. Dana Whitfield stayed on for two years after the acquisition to lead
the combined analytics group before departing to advise startups.
