# The Insight Suite

The Insight Suite is the analytics product built by Vantage Analytics, the company
founded by Dana Whitfield. It provides dashboards and reporting tools that companies
use to visualize operational data.

Whitfield built the Insight Suite while working on large-volume operational data at
Nimbus Systems before founding Vantage Analytics in 2008. The Insight Suite found its
first major market among manufacturers who needed to understand output from factory
equipment.

Ravi Anand spent nearly a decade at Vantage Analytics, eventually becoming one of its
most senior commercial leaders. His familiarity with the Insight Suite directly
influenced Meridian Robotics' decision to acquire Vantage Analytics in 2019, after
Anand had become chief executive at Meridian.

Under Meridian Robotics, the Insight Suite was integrated with the Atlas Arm.
Customers who buy an Atlas Arm can now activate the Insight Suite to see dashboards
showing robot performance, cycle times, and predictive maintenance recommendations.
The combined product is stored and delivered through Nimbus Systems' Stratus platform.

Dana Whitfield led the integration team for two years after the acquisition, making
sure the Insight Suite retained its usability even as it was adapted to robotic
applications.

The Insight Suite has been cited by analysts as an example of enterprise analytics
software that succeeded by being deeply domain-specific: rather than trying to be
a general-purpose analytics tool, it focused on what manufacturers actually needed
to know about their machines, and was rewarded with loyalty that survived
a change of ownership.
