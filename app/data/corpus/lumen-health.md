# Lumen Health

Lumen Health is a medical technology company founded in 2013 by Nadia Cole. Before
starting the company, Nadia Cole earned a PhD at the Calvert Institute of
Technology and then worked as a researcher at Halcyon Labs, the AI lab founded by
Priya Raman.

Lumen Health makes VitalSense, a monitoring product that interprets patient data
to flag early signs of illness. VitalSense is powered by the Oracle Engine, the
large language model built by Halcyon Labs; Nadia Cole's familiarity with the
Oracle Engine from her research years is a large part of why Lumen Health licenses
it rather than building its own model.

The company is frequently cited as an example of talent and technology spreading
outward from Halcyon Labs into new industries. By pairing the Oracle Engine with
medical expertise, Lumen Health brought advanced AI into hospitals that could not
have built such systems on their own.

Nadia Cole and Priya Raman remain in contact, and Lumen Health's continued
reliance on the Oracle Engine keeps the two companies closely linked.
