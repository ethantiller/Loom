# Torch Energy Software

Torch Energy Software was an energy-management software company founded in 2007 by
Elena Sorenson. It built tools that helped large buildings and factories track and
reduce their energy use.

Elena Sorenson was well connected in the Elmsworth technology community and served
as a board member of Nimbus Systems for several years. That relationship helped
set the stage for Torch Energy Software's most important moment.

In 2018 Torch Energy Software was acquired by Nimbus Systems, the cloud company
founded by Priya Raman and Owen Castellano. Nimbus Systems folded Torch's
technology into the Stratus platform as a set of sustainability and
energy-monitoring features, marketed to data-center operators trying to cut power
costs.

After the acquisition, Elena Sorenson continued to advise Nimbus Systems on
sustainability strategy. Torch Energy Software's tools live on inside Stratus,
even though the standalone company no longer exists.
