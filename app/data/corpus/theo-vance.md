# Theo Vance

Theo Vance is a product designer and entrepreneur who founded Cascade Payments after
an early career at Quill.

Vance joined Quill, the productivity software company founded by Marcus Feld, as a
product lead in the late 2000s. Working under Feld, he developed his design
sensibility: he believed that financial software was as needlessly complicated as word
processors had been before the age of simple, consumer-friendly applications. He spent
several years on the Quillpad team before leaving to act on that belief.

In 2011 Vance founded Cascade Payments, a payments processing company aimed at small
businesses. He designed Cascade's products to be as straightforward as Quillpad,
hiding the complexity of payment rails behind clean interfaces. The company secured
early investment from Ashby Capital, the firm run by Leon Ashby, who had also backed
Nimbus Systems and Halcyon Labs.

Cascade Payments grew steadily throughout the 2010s and integrated with Nimbus
Systems' Stratus platform as a cloud-native billing option for Stratus customers.
That integration gave Cascade Payments access to a large base of business users
without a direct sales effort.

Vance has credited Marcus Feld as the most influential figure in his professional
development, saying that Feld's insistence on simplicity over features shaped
everything he built afterward.

Cascade Payments is now one of the more profitable independent fintech companies in
the Elmsworth region, and Vance remains its chief executive.
