# Mara Lindqvist

Mara Lindqvist is the chief executive of Halcyon Labs, the AI research company
founded by Priya Raman.

Lindqvist joined Halcyon Labs in 2015 as chief operating officer after a decade
leading product and operations teams at large technology companies outside the
Elmsworth region. She was known as a rigorous operator who could translate research
output into commercial products.

In 2018 Priya Raman stepped back from the chief executive role at Halcyon Labs
to focus on board responsibilities. Lindqvist was appointed CEO. Under her leadership,
Halcyon Labs expanded its Oracle Engine licensing business and launched a new line of
enterprise APIs that allow companies to fine-tune the Oracle Engine on their own data.

Lindqvist also negotiated the renewal and expansion of Lumen Health's licensing
agreement for the Oracle Engine, securing Halcyon Labs' most significant revenue
relationship through at least 2028.

She brought in a new chief financial officer and built a proper commercial team around
what had previously been a primarily research-focused organization. Colleagues describe
her as the person who transformed Halcyon Labs from a research lab into a sustainable
business without diminishing the quality of its research output.

Priya Raman, who remains chair of the Halcyon Labs board, has praised Lindqvist
publicly for taking Halcyon Labs from a research project to a commercially viable
organization. Lindqvist credits Raman's scientific vision as the foundation she built
on.
