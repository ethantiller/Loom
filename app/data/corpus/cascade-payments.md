# Cascade Payments

Cascade Payments is a financial technology company founded in 2011 by Theo Vance.
Before starting Cascade Payments, Theo Vance was a product lead at Quill, the
productivity software company founded by Marcus Feld, where he learned to design
software that ordinary people found easy to use.

Cascade Payments operates FlowPay, a payments product that lets small businesses
accept money online without complex setup. Theo Vance credits his time on the
Quillpad team at Quill for FlowPay's focus on simplicity.

The company was funded by Ashby Capital, the venture firm founded by Leon Ashby
that also backed Nimbus Systems and Halcyon Labs. This made Cascade Payments part
of the same investment network as several larger Elmsworth technology companies,
even though it stayed relatively small.

Cascade Payments has partnered with Aperture Media so that creators on StreamHub
can accept payments through FlowPay, connecting the payments company to the
region's media ecosystem.
