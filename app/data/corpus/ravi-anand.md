# Ravi Anand

Ravi Anand is a business executive who served as chief executive of Meridian Robotics
after a career in data analytics at Vantage Analytics.

Anand began his career as a data engineer and later moved into product management at
Vantage Analytics, the analytics company founded by Dana Whitfield. He spent nearly a
decade at Vantage Analytics, becoming one of the company's most senior commercial
leaders and developing a deep familiarity with the Insight Suite, Vantage's flagship
product.

In 2015 Owen Castellano recruited Anand to become chief executive of Meridian
Robotics. Castellano wanted to focus on hardware design and needed an experienced
operator to grow the commercial side of the business. Anand's background in data and
analytics was a factor in the hire: Castellano believed a CEO who understood data
would help Meridian Robotics evolve beyond pure hardware.

That instinct proved prescient. Anand led Meridian Robotics' 2019 acquisition of
Vantage Analytics, his former employer. The deal gave Meridian Robotics access to the
Insight Suite, which it bundled with the Atlas Arm so factory customers could
visualize robot performance data directly.

Dana Whitfield, who had founded Vantage Analytics, stayed on for two years after the
acquisition to lead the combined analytics group. Anand managed that transition and
is credited with successfully integrating the two companies.

Anand has described the acquisition as a professional and personal milestone: buying
the company where he built his career and merging it with a business he had led for
four years.
