# Ashby Capital

Ashby Capital is a venture firm founded by Leon Ashby, who also founded and continues
to chair Beacon Semiconductor. The dual role makes Ashby unusual in the Elmsworth
technology community, where investors and operating executives are rarely the same
person.

Ashby Capital focuses on startups that either benefit from the Photon chip's
capabilities or could become significant customers of Beacon Semiconductor's technology.
This investment thesis means the firm's portfolio clusters around infrastructure,
hardware, and capital-intensive software.

Ashby Capital backed Nimbus Systems in its earliest funding round, before the company
had achieved significant scale. Priya Raman and Owen Castellano, Nimbus Systems'
co-founders, have acknowledged that Ashby Capital's early support was critical to
getting Stratus into production. Ashby Capital also invested in Halcyon Labs when
Priya Raman founded the AI research lab in 2012, continuing a relationship with Raman
that had begun at Nimbus Systems.

More recently, Ashby Capital invested in Cascade Payments, the fintech company
founded by Theo Vance, after Cascade Payments demonstrated that it could run its
payments infrastructure efficiently on Stratus and Photon-powered hardware.

Leon Ashby sits on the boards of several Ashby Capital portfolio companies, including
Nimbus Systems and Halcyon Labs. He is known to be more involved in the technical
decisions of portfolio companies than most venture investors — a habit he attributes
to running Beacon Semiconductor throughout his investing career.

Analysts in the Elmsworth region often describe Ashby Capital as the hardware anchor
of the technology cluster, while Northwind Ventures represents the software anchor.
