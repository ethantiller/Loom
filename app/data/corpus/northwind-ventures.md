# Northwind Ventures

Northwind Ventures is a venture capital firm based in Elmsworth. It is one of the
two dominant investors in the regional technology industry, alongside Ashby
Capital.

Northwind Ventures made its name backing consumer and business software companies.
It invested in Quill, the productivity company founded by Marcus Feld; Vantage
Analytics, the data company founded by Dana Whitfield; and Aperture Media, the
streaming company founded by Sofia Marchetti. Northwind Ventures also joined a
later funding round for Nimbus Systems, giving it a stake in the region's largest
cloud provider.

Where Ashby Capital's investments tend to cluster around hardware and the Photon
chip made by Beacon Semiconductor, Northwind Ventures focused more on software and
media. Analysts often describe the Elmsworth startup scene as split between the two
firms' portfolios.

Several Northwind-backed companies were later acquired, including Quill, which
Nimbus Systems bought in 2016, and Vantage Analytics, acquired by Meridian
Robotics in 2019.
